CODE GEASS

One of the flagship that established the reign of Japanese animation. This anime will take you to heart-stopping mecha battles to heartbreaking scenes that will burn to your mind. Join our protagonist Lelouch Lamperouge and see how he rise in the era of darkness. Watch the anime version.

Pandora Hearts

One of the anime and manga that still haunts me until now. A dark-themed fairytale that will explore through mysteries, insanity, and betreyal. Join Oz Bezarius,who have been banished to the depths of the abbys,to search for the sin that cost him his life. He then meets Alice, a familiar who residing at the bottom of the abyys, that is searching for her memory. Will the truth save their sould or would it lead them to a tragedy. Read the Manga Version.

Deadman Wonderland

Also a dark themed anime and manga packed with action and gore. This manga also likes to play with your head with its many twist and turns. Ganta Igarashi have been accused of killing all of his classmates and was sent in to the prison. But this prison is not like any type of prision. Its a cruel world of survival that the prisoners have been under on. Watch him clear his name as he entanggles himself with the mystery of a girl named, Shiro.

Another

This anime will make you feel the chills or close your eyes in disgust. This is not suitable for people who cannot handle explicit death scenes. Wander to the mystery of Class 3-3 of Yomiyama North. This anime is the equivalent of final destination. Join Kouichi and Mei as they try to stop the deaths from happening.

Zetsuzen No Tempest

Inspired by the works of William Shakespeare, especially his play The Tempest, Zetsuen no Tempest is a tragic tale that explores the balance of sanity and madness, sense and intelligence, and self-confidence and convictions. Join Yoshino and Mahiro as they try to survive and solve the mysteries in this deteriorating world

AKB0048

Your girls from the orginal real-life japanese idols have been transported to anime! AKB48 have been turned as AKB0048 as an anime. The setting goes into a world where entertainment are ban from different planets. Now new generation of idols who want to give happiness to people as their idol did. Always smiling and spreading love these girls will compete for the position of the center nova.

Your Lie In April

A music themed anime that will explore to the bonds of friends and lovers. Arima haven't heard the sound of the piano since his mother died. Now Kaori enters his life with her vivid energy and beautiful smile. Can she bring Arima to play in the music that he loved again? This tear jerker of an anime will be sure of a worth it journey.

Gakuen Alice

Don't be fooled by its cute looking animation! Gakuen Alice is a certified tear-jerker of an anime that will affect your life. Mikan is a cute and energetic girl who will study in The School of Alices. The school is actually an elite Academy for gifted people with "Alices," an ability that is unique depending on the individual being. Watch her unfold the mysteries it hold and her connection with it even before she was born. Well to those who will read or watch this I can only wish the best for you.
